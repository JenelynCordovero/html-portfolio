The Best Movies According to Jenelyn

My Top 3 movies of all-time

The Purge

The Purge is my favorite movie because I'm intrigued by its unique concept, enjoy the suspense and thrills it offers, appreciate its social commentary, and find it highly entertaining overall.

Seven Sundays

Seven Sundays resonates with me as my second favorite movie because of its heartfelt exploration of family dynamics, relatable characters, and emotional depth.

Four Sisters and a Wedding

I like the movie "Four Sisters and a Wedding" because of its heartwarming portrayal of sibling relationships, relatable family dynamics, humorous moments, and touching moments of reconciliation and love.
